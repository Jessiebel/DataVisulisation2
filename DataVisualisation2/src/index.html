Understanding Australia's International Travel Trends

Australia, with its stunning landscapes, vibrant cities, and unique wildlife, continues to be a top destination for international travelers. From the early 1990s, Australia experienced steady growth in international visitors, driven by its landscapes, multicultural society, and thriving economy. The 2000 Sydney Olympics and Australia's participation in global events also helped bolster its profile. In recent years, the influx of overseas visitors has become a key contributor to the country's economy. The data explored in this visualisation covers the variety of international arrivals in Australia including their country of citizenship, visa type and state/teritory of arrival.

Note: "Arrivals" includes Australian citizens returning home as well as international visitors
